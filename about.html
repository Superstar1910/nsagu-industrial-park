About Nsagu Industrial Park

Nsagu Industrial Park is a 5-acre master-planned industrial estate located in Nsagu, Wakiso, designed as Uganda’s first SME-focused Grade-A industrial hub outside Kampala.

The park is developed in phases, beginning with a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) — two modular warehouses, a gatehouse, basic utilities, and truck circulation areas — to validate tenant demand before scaling to full build-out.

Key Park Advantages

  • 5-acre expandable industrial site
  • Modern, secure, professionally managed space
  • Strategic location between Kampala and Entebbe
  • Affordable rents for SMEs
  • DFI-ready governance and structuring
Location

Strategic Nsagu–Nankonge Corridor

Positioned near Newman Foods Factory, the park offers an ideal balance of accessibility, affordability, and future industrial growth potential.

  • 25 minutes to Entebbe Expressway (Kawuku)
  • 1 hour to Entebbe International Airport
  • 45–70 minutes to Kampala
  • Rich labour pool from Nsagu–Kitende–Kajjansi corridor
