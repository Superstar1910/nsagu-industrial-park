Investment-Grade Industrial Platform

Nsagu Industrial Park is structured to attract private investors, corporate partners, and development finance institutions (DFIs) through a well-governed SPV, clear land rights, and phased capital deployment.

Key Numbers

  • Phase 1 CapEx: UGX 800m – 1.1bn
  • GLA: ~700 m²
  • Target Occupancy Year 1: 80%
  • Projected Multi-Phase IRR: 18–24%

Legal & Capital Structure

The project uses a clean, bankable structure that protects the landowner, reduces tax leakage, and enables institutional investment.

  • Land: Private Mailo, owned by individual
  • Long-term registered lease to SPV
  • SPV owned by TPL (developer) & Oakstone (financial sponsor)
  • Reserved matters and governance committees
